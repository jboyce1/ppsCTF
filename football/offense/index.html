TA0043 Reconnaissance

Like scouting opponents' games, watching tapes, and learning about their key players. This is gathering intel to plan future plays and strategies.

TA0042 Resource Development

Similar to off-season training, recruiting new players, and developing playbooks. It's about building and improving the team's capabilities and strategies for future games.

TA0001 Initial Access

This can be likened to a team's first drive in a game, where the objective is to penetrate the opponent's defense and gain ground, akin to an adversary trying to breach a network.

TA0002 Execution

Comparable to executing a specific play in football. It's the actual carrying out of a planned action, whether it's a running play, a pass, or a special teams play.

TA0003 Persistence

This is akin to maintaining possession of the ball, continually advancing downfield play after play, much like an adversary wanting to maintain their presence in a system.

TA0004 Privilege Escalation

In football terms, this could be seen as gaining a strategic advantage, like moving from a standard play to a more dominant position on the field, or exploiting a weakness in the opposing team's defense.

TA0005 Defense Evasion

This is like using deceptive plays, like a play-action pass or a double reverse, to avoid being read and countered by the defense.

TA0006 Credential Access

This could be compared to intercepting communications or stealing signals from the opposing team, gaining insights into their plans and movements.

TA0007 Discovery

Similar to analyzing the opponent's formation and tactics during a game to understand their strategy and adjust your plays accordingly.

TA0008 Lateral Movement

This is akin to the ball being passed around among team members, finding the best path through the defense to advance towards the goal.

TA0009 Collection

Comparable to a team gathering data on their performance, such as yards gained, to inform future plays or strategy adjustments.

TA0011 Command and Control

This can be seen as the quarterback or coach directing the team, calling plays, and making adjustments based on the game's progress.

TA0010 Exfiltration

In football, this could be likened to a team taking possession of the ball (through an interception or fumble recovery) and moving it into their control to score.

TA0040 Impact

This is similar to plays that disrupt or impact the opposing team's strategy, like a sack, a forced fumble, or a block, which directly affect the game's flow and the opponent's ability to execute their game plan.
